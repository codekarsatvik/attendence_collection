    Attendence Portal

    It is a django based project where teacher can upload the attendence
    sheet of students and perform data manipulation on the same

    Features

    Upload Attendence

    Teachers of particular class can upload attendence sheet of their particular class and subject

    Login Encription

    Teachers and students can signup to the attendence portal with safe encription

    Separate Dash

    There are two seperate portals for teachers as well as students where teachers can upload attendence,add new subject,class while students can only view the detailed description of the attendence

    Filters

    There are various filters offered by portal like subject wise filter,class wise filter and date wise filter

    How to use

    For Teachers

    {% comment %}

    A highly skilled professional service with a creative touch.

    {% endcomment %}

    Step 1

    Sign up yourself as a teacher and then login your credentials.

    Step 2

    Add your class and Subject

    Step 3

    Upload the attendence excel file by selecting your class and subjects and then confirm.

    Step 4

    At teacher dashboard you will see your attendences uploaded.You can also filter it according to date,subject and class.

    For Students


    Step 1

    Sign up yourself as a Student and then login your credentials.

    Step 2

    At student dashboard you will see your attendence where you can also filter it according to date and subject.
